Welcome to the Bulls and Cows game!
    Rules:
  • The program thinks of 4 differents numbers.
  • Your task is to guess values of numbers and their places in a row: you enter 4 differents numbers and the program tells you how many bulls and cows have you got.
  • If you guessed only a value of a number right (which means this number is in a row but not on this place), you'll get a cow. If you guessed both a value and a place of a number you'll get a bull.
  • Collect 4 bulls and you'll be the winner!
  • Attention: you have only 8 attempts.
